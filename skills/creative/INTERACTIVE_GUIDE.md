## Implementation Paths

### Path 1: E-Commerce (Product-Focused)

**Goal**: Create professional product visuals for online store

**Sequence**:
1. **Creative Strategist** ‚Äî Define product photography aesthetic
   - Answer: What's your brand aesthetic?
   - Output: Visual direction guide
   
2. **Image Generation** ‚Äî Learn FAL.ai image generation
   - Ask: How do I generate product images?
   - Output: Test images, refined prompts
   
3. **Product Photography** ‚Äî Create product shots
   - Ask: Generate product photography for [product]
   - Output: Product images for store
   
4. **Social Graphics** ‚Äî Create promotional graphics
   - Ask: Generate Instagram post for [product]
   - Output: Social media graphics
   
5. **Brand Asset** ‚Äî Create brand elements
   - Ask: Generate logo for [brand]
   - Output: Brand assets
   
6. **Product Video** ‚Äî Plan product videos
   - Ask: Plan a product reveal video for [product]
   - Output: Video concept and storyboard

**Timeline**: 8-12 weeks

**Deliverables**:
- 20-50 product photos
- 10-20 social graphics
- Brand logo and icon set
- Product video concepts
